Stem At Exeter


College and career readiness for a changing world.

Supported by industry and community partners, the STEM program at Exeter

Township Senior High School aims to support students with innovative and

relevant curricular options that will nurture creative and critical

thinking and problem solving skills specific to college and career

pathways in the fields of science, technology, engineering and mathematics.
